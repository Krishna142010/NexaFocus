⚡ NexaFocus — AI-Powered Study Productivity App

NexaFocus is a full-stack student productivity web app built with React + Vite, 
powered by Google Gemini 2.0 Flash AI and backed by Supabase for authentication 
and cloud sync. It combines smart study sessions, AI assistance, gamified 
milestones, and advanced animations to make studying engaging, accountable, 
and rewarding.

Built for students who want more than a timer — NexaFocus tracks your focus, 
celebrates your wins, and keeps you on track with an AI study partner available 
24/7.
